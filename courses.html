DATA SCIENCE PROFESSIONAL COURSES ROADMAP

Advance your journey with our step-by-step courses. Each course is 14.99 USD — designed for practical mastery from foundations to freelancing.

Level 4: Deep Learning & AI

  • Generative AI (GANs, prompt engineering, fine-tuning models)

Level 5: Advanced Topics & Specializations

  • Big Data & Data Engineering: Apache Spark, Hadoop, Airflow
  • Cloud platforms: AWS, GCP, Azure for data pipelines
  • Time Series Analysis & Forecasting
  • Recommender Systems
  • MLOps & Deployment: Model serving, monitoring, CI/CD pipelines

Level 6: Applied Data Science

  • Case Studies in Business, Finance, Healthcare, Marketing
  • Data Ethics & Responsible AI

Level 7: Professional Growth

  • Building a Portfolio & GitHub Projects
  • Technical Writing & Blogging for Data Scientists
  • Interview Preparation (Leetcode for DS, system design for ML)
